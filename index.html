Controls
  • a/ ArrowLeft : Left Movement
  • d/ ArrowRight : Right Movement
  • s/ ArrowDown : Move Down
  • w/ ArrowUp : Rotation of Shapes
  • space bar : pause
  • m : mute/unmute

  • TOUCH CONTROLS:
  • Swipe Left : Left Movement
  • Swipe Right : Right Movement
  • Swipe Down : Move Down
  • Swipe Up : Rotation of Shapes
